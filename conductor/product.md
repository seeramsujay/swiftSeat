# Product: SwiftSeat

## One-Line Description
The unified, Gemini-powered operations and concierge platform for stadium logistics and fan experience.

## Problem Statement
Large-scale venues suffer from inefficient crowd management, long concession queues, and poor user guidance, leading to safety risks and lost revenue.

## Solution
SwiftSeat leverages the **PALO Algorithm** and **Gemini AI** to provide predictive routing, real-time density monitoring, and a seamless food ordering experience through a unified platform for Fans, Vendors, and Administrators.

## Target User Personas
- **The Fan**: Wants to spend less time in line and more time in their seat.
- **The Vendor**: Needs efficient order management and inventory transparency.
- **The Administrator**: Requires a bird's-eye view of venue safety and logistics.

## Core Features
1. **PALO Routing Engine**: Predictive arrival-time optimization to balance loads across stands.
2. **Gemini Concierge**: Multimodal AI for natural language routing and ordering.
3. **Heatmap & Telemetry**: Live IoT feed of stadium density and hardware health.
4. **Operations Command Center**: Role-based oversight and emergency controls.

## Success Metrics
- Average Queue Time Reduction: > 30%
- Revenue Lift per Stand: > 15%
- Venue Safety Compliance: 100% (No over-capacity zones)
